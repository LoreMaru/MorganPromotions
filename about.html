Morgan Promotions



Connecting people

As simple as that.
By nature my extroverted behaviour had always generated loads of connections between different kind of people.
People with something in common: The Love for the Music. Techno Music.
I've started with a bunch of friends going from club to another, spending days...nights...periods of time! Then I realized that we are One.
That we need each other. That a DJ, without a dancing crowd is nothing, as a crowd without a playing DJ.
Same as for locations, mixer, smoking machine and...promoters.
Also the best clubs, with the greatest DJ's, could collapse without a solid foundation of promotions. This is where Morgan Promotions was born.
From the idea to connect people to events and viceversa. WhatsApp groups, Facebook events, Instagram spots...I wanted more.
I wanted a North Star to guide us in the darkest hour.
When you think that there is nothing more that night, when you feel that your soul could dance more and your heartbeat is still high...I'm your man.
Because I've been there many times, and in one of those moments I found my direction by mistake (or was it my fate?)
Corsica Studios, Jaded. Not just an after..."My first After". This inspired me, showed me different kind of community and people, with love and respect, people who love to look after each others.
People that were telling me "hey, outside there is more". And guess what? There were more indeed.
More clubs, more people, more events basically more of Us.
I went at Cafe1001 for the "free techno sunday" with plenty of friends there, keep going and going. My mental horizons started to increase perspectives, I went to Fold for UNfold and...Bang! My people, my crowd.
Big click and a bigger Love. And then Village Underground, Electrowerkz, The Cause, Pickle Factory, clubs that do really love, for events that were able to make my week, my month...my Life.
Events like Jaded, UNfold, HTBX, Inferno, Kaos, Boudica, Unmasked, Corner, Noise Orchestra, Aria, Rave After Rave, Sabbath, Superstition and more.
So I realized tha the Underground Techno Clubs are built on a different concept than just Music.
Are built on real Love for each others, without prejudice, stereotypes or ignorance. There is no room for Ego there...just pure souls carried by lovely bodies.
No gender, no discrimination...because "labels are for clothes".
I started to feel, not just see. I started getting to know some DJ's, becoming friends of them, knowing some secret location in warehouses, some...Underground Raves.
This is a philosophy of Love & Respect, I'm not representing a Club here, I am not an Agent of some DJ's...I'm a Raver, just like You.
I am who will connect the dots in the darkest highest night...or day!
Because it is not just about dancing, it is not just the music, it is "That" Feeling.
A rhythmic energy from vibrations and frequencies created from sounds and souls. Generating unity and the experience of oneness...Its cosmic.
So when in need...I'll make it possible for you to book some event, I'll ask to some DJ where they will perform, I'll tell you where to find a reduced wristbands for some after and I will guide you to the Magic.
Because that's not my job, that's my vocation.
